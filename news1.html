Notre Dame Cathedral Caught on Fire

Written By: TyeMonkey

Today

The famous Notre Dame Cathedral of France recently caught on fire with many witnesses on scene. Reports say a man was on top of the building after hours and lit a spark. This spark then caused a fire and ended up getting the entire top center of the Cathedral on fire. Firetrucks quickly reported on scene to put out the fire. Although it took some time to put out the massive fire, firemen were able to put it out. People belive the game Assassins Creed France could be used as a guide to rebuild the Cathedral.
